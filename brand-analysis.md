# Ocean Armour Brand Identity & Positioning Analysis

## Executive Summary
Ocean Armour is a specialized marine coating service company positioned as the licensed dealer and certified application partner for Aquaphobix thermoplastic powder coating technology in South Florida (Broward and Palm Beach Counties). The brand emphasizes professional-grade marine protection with environmental responsibility, high performance, and shipyard expertise.

---

## 1. Primary Business & Brand Definition

### What is Ocean Armour?
Ocean Armour is a **professional-grade marine coating application service** that specializes in applying Aquaphobix technology to boat hulls and marine vessels. The company operates as a licensed dealer and certified team serving the Broward and Palm Beach County regions.

### Core Business Model:
- **Service**: Hull coating application for vessels of all sizes
- **Technology**: Proprietary Aquaphobix thermoplastic powder coating
- **Delivery**: Professional shipyard-grade application and prep work
- **Geography**: Licensed to serve Broward and Palm Beach Counties
- **Customer Base**: Boat owners seeking premium hull protection and extended haul-out intervals

### Brand Promise:
**"Hull Protection That Works"** - Professional-grade Aquaphobix coating system for boats that demand durability, performance, and protection.

---

## 2. Relationship Between Ocean Armour & Aquaphobix

### Aquaphobix Role:
Aquaphobix is the **product/technology manufacturer** that develops and provides the marine coating system. It's a specialized thermoplastic powder coating technology engineered specifically for marine environments.

### Ocean Armour Role:
Ocean Armour is the **licensed dealer and certified service provider** in South Florida who applies Aquaphobix technology. The company does NOT manufacture the product but rather:
- Partners with Aquaphobix as an authorized dealer
- Applies the system using certified technicians
- Provides professional shipyard-grade preparation and application
- Handles customer service, consultation, and support

### Positioning Relationship:
Throughout the site, the relationship is presented as:
- **"Ocean Armour is the licensed Aquaphobix dealer"** (primary identity)
- Aquaphobix is prominently featured in the header with a badge: "Licensed Dealer | Aquaphobix | Professional Coating System"
- The Aquaphobix logo and branding are highlighted in the hero section with a dedicated endorsement card
- In footer and other sections, Ocean Armour clearly states: "Certified Aquaphobix team delivering non-toxic, high-performance protection"

### Key Messaging Pattern:
Ocean Armour = **Local expertise + Professional service + Aquaphobix technology**

## 3. Brand Positioning Throughout the Site

### Primary Positioning Strategy
The brand is positioned at the intersection of **three key pillars**:

#### A. Professional Excellence
- "Shipyard-grade" application and prep
- "Certified team" with documented quality control
- Professional haul-out, cleaning, blasting, and surface preparation
- Final inspection and documentation

#### B. Environmental Responsibility
- "Zero toxic biocides" (clean waterways messaging)
- "No heavy metals or leaching"
- "Compliance with evolving environmental regulations"
- Eco-friendly alternative to traditional antifouling paints

#### C. Performance & Durability
- 5-year warranty
- 850 PSI bond strength
- 3000-hour UV tested
- Power-washable surfaces
- Hydrodynamic efficiency (reduced drag)

### Brand Architecture:
```
Ocean Armour (Service Company)
    ↓
"Licensed Dealer & Certified Team"
    ↓
Aquaphobix Technology (Product)
    ↓
Three Value Pillars: Professional + Environmental + Performance
```
